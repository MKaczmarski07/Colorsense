What do those numbers mean? Following the Web Content Accesibility Guidelines (WCAG), the required minimum contrast ratio (AA) for normal text is at least 4.5:1, and at least 3:1 for large or bold text. The standard also defines an enhanced contrast ratio (AAA), which is at least 7:1 for normal text and at least 4.5:1 for large or bold text. Adhering to WCAG guidelines regarding contrast is essential to ensure that web content is always readable and accessible for all users, regardless of their visual abilities
